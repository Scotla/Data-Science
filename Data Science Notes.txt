Introduction to Data Science

Data can be:
Qualitative -  Descriptive - non-numerical data such as reviews

Quantitative - Numerical - can be:

1. Discreet - represented in whole numbers- includes counts of things such:
- number of people
- number of transactions

2. Continuous data - represented in many ways, not necessarily whole numbers
It includes:
- Average distance travelled by a certain car in a certain period of time
